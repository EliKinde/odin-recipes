Air Fryer Tortilla Quiche

Description

This air fryer tortilla quiche is quick and easy to prepare.
You can add any toppings or seasonings you like.

Ingredients

  • air fryer liner
  • 1 large tortilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • salt and feshly ground black papper
  • 1/2 cup spinach torn into pieces
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded ceese

Steps

  1. Line your air fryer with an air fryer liner. Place tortilla into the air fryer, so the sides are covered.
  2. Whisk eggs and cottage cheese together in a bowl. Pour into the tortilla in the air fryer and season
    with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with bell pepper and spinach. Top with cheese.
  3. Air fry at 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) until eggs are set,
    about 20 minutes. Remove, allow to cool for a few minutes, quarter, and serve.
