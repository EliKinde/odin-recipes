No-Rise Pizza Dough

Description

This quick pizz dough recipe simply involves mixing a few basic
ingredients and patting the dough into the pan for a delicious curst

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 oackage active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.
    Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes togther
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees C.
  4. dough into a large pizza pan
  5. cover with pizza sauce and top with peppernoi slice and shradded cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheat oven until crust is lightky browned 20 to 25 minutes.
