Green Power Smoothie

Description

This tart satisfying and delicious green smoothi.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups ice cubes
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 can crushed pineapple
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 banana
  • 1 orange
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Blend ice, spinach, pineapple, water, banana, orange, mint, lemon juice, blender until smooth.
